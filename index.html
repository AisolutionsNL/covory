COVORY

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. Covory is designed to help you manage receipts, returns, refunds and warranties while keeping your information under your control.

2. Information You Store

Covory does not require you to create an account.

The information you choose to store may include:

  • receipt and purchase information;
  • store and item names;
  • purchase, return and warranty dates;
  • refund information;
  • notes;
  • receipt photographs;
  • PDF copies and scans of receipts.

3. Local Storage

Covory stores your receipt data and associated files locally on your device. Covory does not upload your receipts or personal information to servers operated by us.

4. Optional In-App Purchases

Covory may offer optional voluntary support purchases through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Payments are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment-card details.

Apple may provide transaction information needed to confirm that a purchase was completed.

5. Analytics, Advertising and Tracking

Covory does not use third-party advertising, does not sell your personal information and does not track you across apps or websites.

Covory currently does not use third-party analytics services to build profiles of your activity.

6. Notifications

If you grant permission, Covory may schedule local notifications for return deadlines, refunds and warranties. These reminders are scheduled on your device.

7. Camera, Photos and Files

Covory may request access to your camera, photo library or files only when you choose to photograph, scan, select or import a receipt. These permissions are used solely to provide the feature you request.

8. Data Sharing

Covory does not sell or rent your data. Information leaves the app only when you deliberately use an export or sharing feature, or when Apple processes an optional In-App Purchase.

9. Data Deletion

You may delete receipts and their associated information within Covory. Removing Covory from your device also removes locally stored app data, subject to the normal behavior of Apple's operating systems and any backups you control.

10. Children's Privacy

Covory is not specifically directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when Covory's features or legal requirements change. The current version will remain available on this page.

12. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to: aisolutionsnl@outlook.com
